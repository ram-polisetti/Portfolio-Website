About Me

Hello! I’m Ram, a passionate and driven Data Scientist dedicated to transforming complex data into actionable insights that drive decision-making and innovation. With a Master’s degree in Data Science University at Buffalo - SUNY, I have honed my skills in statistical analysis, machine learning, and predictive modeling.

Over the past 2 years, I have worked with diverse teams in E-commerce, Supply Chain, Retail and HR, where I have leveraged data to solve real-world problems. My projects range from developing sophisticated algorithms that predict consumer behavior to implementing machine learning models that improve healthcare outcomes.

I am proficient in Python, R, and SQL and am familiar with essential data science tools and libraries such as Pandas, Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow, and Keras. I’m also experienced in data visualization tools like Tableau and PowerBI, which help me turn complex results into compelling stories.

When I’m not crunching numbers, I’m exploring the latest in artificial intelligence, attending tech meetups, or blogging about data science trends to share my knowledge and learn from others in the community.

I am excited to bring my expertise in data science to a dynamic team that values innovation and collaboration. Let’s connect and discuss how we can work together to drive your business forward!
